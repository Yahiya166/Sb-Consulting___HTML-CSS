Projects

Turn Key Projects for new Entrepreneurs and existing medium to large scale food industries

SB Consulting offers the highest level of professional food science, technology and engineering support, equipment layout design engineering and project management consultancy service and advice.

Our staff approach every job with commitment; a primary focus to understand the client’s needs and the delivery of professional and cost-effective engineering solutions for all scale of industries.

Our vision is to be the leading provider of one window food technology consulting services in Pakistan and GCC to both public and private clients.

The business is led by a senior Food Technologist with an excellent track record of 30 years in the delivery of mega and medium scale infrastructure food/FMCG projects within the public and private sectors.

Technical Support is provided by a team of food technologists, new product development technicians, electrical, mechanical, and civil engineers for erection, installation, and commissioning support for all scale of projects.

The adequate consideration of health and safety, supply chain and IT services is an integral part of our approach to the investigation, design, and delivery of our projects.

SB Consulting can help in implementation of a defined quality management system under the framework of BS EN ISO 9001, 14000, 18000, 22000
